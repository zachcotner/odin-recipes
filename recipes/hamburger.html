Hamburger

This is a McDonald's Hamburger (without ketchup because it only belongs on fries)

    Ingredients

  • Hamburger Bun (1)
  • Ground Beed (1/5 lb.)
  • Onion (diced)
  • Pickels (4 slices)
  • Mustard to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

    Method

  1. Press you ground beef into a patty
  2. Season your patty with salt and pepper
  3. Place ground beef onto grill that has been brought to temp
  4. Place cooked patty onto bottom bun
  5. Top your patty with the vegetables
  6. Spread mustard onto top bun
  7. Place top bun onto the veggies
  8. Eat
