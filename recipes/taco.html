Taco Recipe

This recipe will show you how to make a ground beef soft taco.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef (one pound)
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Tomato (1)
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Optional: Sour Cream, Guacamole

    1. Cook the ground beef
    2. place the ground beef in warmed tortilla
    3. top with condiment
    4. Eat
